Personal Branding


Working on my own personal brand was a difficult one, there are so many options and ways you can go it can be difficult to narrow it down.


After a while going to and fro I settled on a clean modern look with a geometric logo and sleek lettering. Bebas Neue is possibly my favourite font and I think it really worked well with the logo. I did however, like experimenting with other comical fonts.


I applied the brand to a number of mockups to show how it could work across a variety of mediums.
