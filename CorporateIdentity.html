Corporate Identity


Design a corporate brand, which includes, logo, font choices, colour pallet, book covers and an icon set.


Icon generation is not one of my strong suits but it still was a good project, it introduced me to Adobe Illustrator and vectors which is an extrememly useful skill.


My favourite part was creating the logo and the book covers. I loved the challenge; that we had to create a selection of book covers that all fit in together as a series. Part of the aim was to make people want to buy them. I felt that if the books not only had cohesive covers, but rather covers that all combined to create a bigger image, that would encourage people to buy the whole set to complete the image.
