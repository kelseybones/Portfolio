ABOUT ME


My mother was an artist.

My father was a physicist.

I guess when you put those two things together you get a web designer.


I have a love for all things design and in a perfect world I would clone myself and send myself off to work in photography, architecture, graphic design and about a hundred other creative fields. Unfortunately I can’t do that and thus I had to choose just one area, so I choose web design, With a bit of graphic design and illustration thrown in for good measure..


If you’re looking for someone who gets excited about the perfect font pairing and slick micro interactions then I’m who you’re looking for.
