Wee Weather App


A university project, design a functioning weather app using Javascript, html and css.


I know, everyone and their dog has designed a weather app at some point. So how is mine different? Well I really didn't make it to tell you the weather well. I made it to make you smile, even when it's raining.


We were given a challenge, to design and make a weather app, to make it our own and to make it different. The main reason behind this project was to introduce us to Javascript, so while their was a large learning curve with this project, I really enjoyed it.
